Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are they to scale?

A: Nope! That might be an interesting future project, but the sizes are too different. Rhode island has the smallest border perimeter at 143 miles, compared to alaska at 8,178 miles. So if we only took one mile for Rhode Island (actually did in 8.5 miles), then Alaska would be an ultra marathon of 57 miles.

Q: How are you going to do Hawaii?

A: Not a clue. It's been stumping us for a while. Even if you pause the tracking, it just creates a straight line in between where you pause and resume (a feature we often use when it's not possible to cut through a building). We're open to suggestions.

Q: How do you come up with the maps?

A: We plan the route ahead of time using MapMyRun (but there are plenty others). You can sometimes eyeball where it would fit; otherwise, we'll start with a map of burlington, and then overlay a blank state map. If you paste it MSPaint, you can choose 'transparent selection', which will get rid of all the white.
